### **F-07.3 Transactional Email System**

**Summary**

All system-triggered emails (registration, order confirmation, dispatch, refund, etc.) are dispatched via SendGrid/SES through BullMQ queues with retry logic.

**Business Rules**

| BR \# | Rule |
| :---- | :---- |
| BR-01 | TC-29: Email retry policy: 3 attempts at 1-minute intervals, then 1 final attempt at 15 minutes. After 4 failures: dead-letter queue. Alert sent to admin. |
| BR-02 | All emails rendered from versioned templates stored in DB. Template changes do not require a deployment. |
| BR-03 | Unsubscribe link included in all marketing-adjacent emails per CAN-SPAM / GDPR. Transactional emails (order confirm, dispatch, refund) are exempt but include account settings link. |
