## M05 — Order Management

Covers the full lifecycle of a placed order: confirmation, history, tracking, and admin fulfilment. Orders are created exclusively via the Stripe webhook (F-04.2) and progress through a defined status machine.

---

### F-05.1 Order Confirmation & History

**Summary**

Customers receive an order confirmation email immediately after payment, and can view their full order history and order details — including configuration detail and confirmed lead times — from their account.

**User Story**

As a customer who just paid, I want to see a confirmation page and receive a confirmation email with my order details and confirmed lead times so that I know exactly what I ordered and when to expect delivery.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. Stripe webhook (payment_intent.succeeded) received → order record created with status='confirmed'.
2. 2. Customer redirected to /order-confirmation/:orderId with full order summary including configuration details and lead times per item.
   3. 3. Confirmation email (E-03) dispatched via BullMQ immediate job. TC-29 retry policy applies.
      4. 4. Customer visits /account/orders to view full order history — paginated, most recent first.
         5. 5. Customer clicks an order → /account/orders/:orderId shows itemised receipt with configuration detail, status, tracking link (when dispatched).
           
            6. **Flow — Edge Cases**
           
            7. | Edge Case | Expected System Behaviour |
            8. |---|---|
            9. | Customer refreshes /order-confirmation after session cleared | Order found by orderId in URL if customer is authenticated and owns the order. |
            10. | Guest order (OD-01) | Confirmation page accessible via one-time URL. Email sent. |
            11. | Webhook delayed — order not yet created | Page polls GET /orders/:orderId up to 30 seconds. If still missing: 'Your order is being processed — check your email for confirmation.' |
           
            12. **Business Rules**
           
            13. | BR # | Rule |
            14. |---|---|
            15. | BR-01 | Order history: all orders for authenticated customer, paginated (default 20/page), sorted by created_at DESC. |
            16. | BR-02 | Order detail includes: line items (product name, configuration summary, qty, unit price, line total), lead time per line, subtotal, trade discount, shipping, tax, total, delivery address, status, tracking number. |
            17. | BR-03 | TC-17: Confirmation email dispatched as immediate BullMQ job. Retry: TC-29 (3× at 1 min, 1× at 15 min). |
            18. | BR-04 | Order reference format: HS-[YEAR]-[6-digit sequential ID] (e.g. HS-2026-004821). |
