### F-10.2 Trade Pricing Tiers Management

**Summary**

Trade Admins (and Super Admin) define pricing tiers (e.g. Trade-Standard, Trade-Pro, Trade-Architect) with a percentage-off-list discount and category-level overrides, plus per-customer pricing overrides for strategic accounts. Each Verified Trade Account is assigned a tier (default chosen at approval per F-10.1). The runtime catalogue (F-02.x), cart (F-03.x), and checkout calculator (F-04.4) all read tier resolution at request time.

**Background / Context**

BRD §8.4 lists "manage trade pricing tiers" as Phase-1 scope. BRD §3 (Persona 2 — Interior Architect, Persona 3 — Corporate Buyer) calls out that trade pricing must be invisible to non-trade roles (BR-003 of master) and must respect VAT-exemption rules for EU accounts (BR-004). F-10.2 is the producer of the pricing rules; F-04.4 (and product display surfaces) consume them.

**User Story**

As a Trade Admin, I want to define pricing tiers and per-account overrides so that approved trade customers see the right price at every surface — PDP, cart, checkout — without ad-hoc engineering involvement.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. Admin opens `/admin/trade/pricing` (SCR-TR-04 / SCR-TA-10..12). Tabs: **Tiers**, **Customer Overrides**, **Effective Pricing Preview**.
2. **Tiers tab**: list of tiers with name, baseline `discount_percent`, # accounts on tier, status.
3. Admin **Create Tier**: name, baseline percent (0–60%), category overrides (per category override percent), currency-specific rounding rule, status.
4. **Customer Overrides tab**: search Verified Trade Accounts; for an account, set per-product or per-category percent override that supersedes their tier baseline.
5. **Effective Pricing Preview**: admin enters customer + product → system shows list price, tier discount, override (if any), final trade price, EU VAT-exempt flag, and citation of which rule applied.
6. On save, all caches (CDN PDP cache, cart pricing cache) invalidated for affected scope; new pricing live within 60s. Locked checkout sessions retain prior pricing (TC-14).
7. All write actions audited.

**Flow — Edge Cases**

| Edge Case | Expected System Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Tier baseline > 60% | 422; cap configurable Super-Admin only. |
| Customer override that yields a higher price than tier baseline | Allowed (e.g. tier baseline 30%, override 25% — used for strategic discount cap). Admin warned with comparison. |
| Tier deleted while accounts still assigned | Block delete; UI shows count of assignees and forces reassignment. |
| Override deleted | Customer reverts to tier baseline; preview reflects immediately. |
| Concurrent edit (two admins on same tier) | Optimistic locking; second writer 409. |
| Non-trade customer hits trade-pricing endpoint | 403; trade pricing is server-side gated (BR-003 of master). |
| Currency rounding produces .005 cases | Round half-up by default; per-currency override allowed. |

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | Trade pricing is server-resolved at every read. Never computed client-side and never sent to non-trade roles. |
| BR-02 | Resolution order (highest precedence first): (1) per-customer per-product override, (2) per-customer per-category override, (3) tier per-category override, (4) tier baseline. |
| BR-03 | Final trade price = `list_price × (1 − resolved_percent)`, then rounded per currency rule. |
| BR-04 | Pricing changes are non-retroactive. Locked checkout sessions (TC-14) and confirmed orders use the price captured at lock time. |
| BR-05 | EU VAT-exemption (BR-004 of master) applies to *checkout VAT*, not to the trade-price line item. Trade price is always net-of-list-discount; VAT exemption is layered on at F-04.4. |
| BR-06 | Tier and override edits trigger cache invalidation for affected SKUs / customers. |
| BR-07 | Spec PDFs at full resolution remain restricted to Verified Trade Accounts (BR-008 of master) — pricing surface does not relax that rule. |
| BR-08 | All write actions audited; preview reads are not. |

**Input Validation Rules**

| Field | Type | Required | Validation Rules | Error Message Shown |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| tier_name | String | Yes | 3–48 chars; unique per env. | 'Tier name in use.' |
| baseline_percent | Decimal(5,2) | Yes | 0–60 (config-bounded). | 'Discount must be 0–60%.' |
| category_override_percent | Decimal(5,2) | No | 0–80. | 'Override must be 0–80%.' |
| customer_override_percent | Decimal(5,2) | Yes (override) | 0–80. | 'Override must be 0–80%.' |
| currency_rounding | Enum | Yes | `half_up`, `half_even`, `floor` | 'Select rounding rule.' |
| status | Enum | Yes | `active`, `archived` | 'Invalid status.' |
