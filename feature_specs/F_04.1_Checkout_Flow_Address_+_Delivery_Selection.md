## M04 — Checkout & Payment

The checkout and payment module handles the full purchase completion flow — from TC-13 inventory hold creation through payment processing and order creation. It is the most business-critical module: every friction point here directly reduces revenue.

---

### F-04.1 Checkout Flow (Address + Delivery Selection)

**Summary**

Multi-step checkout collecting delivery address and method — including white-glove delivery selection for large furniture. Creates TC-13: 15-minute inventory hold and enforces TC-14: 30-minute checkout session hard expiry. Lead times are locked per configured item at checkout initiation.

**Background / Context**

For premium design furniture, checkout carries additional complexity vs. standard e-commerce: white-glove delivery eligibility must be surfaced and selected here, lead times must be displayed and locked per configuration, and the inventory hold timer must be prominent given the high-consideration purchase behaviour.

**User Story**

As a customer ready to buy, I want a clear checkout where I enter my delivery address, choose my delivery method (including white-glove if available), and see confirmed lead times so I can complete my purchase confidently.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. Customer clicks 'Proceed to Checkout' from cart.
2. 2. TC-13: System creates inventory_hold records in Redis (atomic SETNX) for each cart item. TC-14: 30-min checkout session created in DB.
   3. 3. Step 1 — Address: customer enters/selects delivery address. System validates serviceability.
      4. 4. Step 2 — Delivery: system returns available methods with prices, estimated dates, and white-glove option if eligible. Lead time per configured item displayed and locked.
         5. 5. Customer selects method → summary shown (items, lead times, shipping, estimated tax) → proceeds to payment (F-04.2).
            6. 6. TC-13: Countdown timer shown when ≤5 minutes remain.
              
               7. **Flow — Edge Cases**
              
               8. | Edge Case | Expected System Behaviour |
               9. |---|---|
               10. | TC-13: Hold expires (15 min) | Hold released. 'Your reservation expired.' Redirect to /cart. |
               11. | TC-14: Full session expires (30 min) | 'Your checkout session expired. Please start again.' Redirect to /cart. All holds released. |
               12. | Item goes out of stock between hold and payment | Caught at PaymentIntent creation. Item-level error shown. Return to cart. |
               13. | Customer navigates away mid-checkout | Hold remains active until TC-13 TTL. Customer can return within window. |
               14. | White-glove only available for some items | Split delivery option offered: white-glove for large items + standard for small accessories. |
              
               15. **Business Rules**
              
               16. | BR # | Rule |
               17. |---|---|
               18. | BR-01 | TC-13: Inventory hold via Redis SETNX (atomic). TTL: 15 minutes. Other customers' checkout for same configuration sees reduced available qty. |
               19. | BR-02 | TC-14: 30-min checkout session enforced server-side. Prevents stale prices, lead times, and tax on long-idle checkouts. |
               20. | BR-03 | White-glove delivery: product weight_kg > admin-configured threshold AND destination ZIP in white-glove service zone (CR-11). |
               21. | BR-04 | Lead time per configured item locked at checkout session creation. Displayed prominently in delivery step and order summary. |
               22. | BR-05 | Guest checkout proceeds without account if OD-01 approved. Guest order linked to email provided. |
              
               23. **Input Validation Rules**
              
               24. | Field | Type | Required | Validation Rules | Error Message Shown |
               25. |---|---|---|---|---|
               26. | addressLine1 | String | Yes | Min 5, max 200 chars. | 'Address is required' |
               27. | city | String | Yes | Min 2, max 100 chars. | 'City is required' |
               28. | zip / postalCode | String | Yes | US: 5 digits or ZIP+4. EU: 4-6 digits. UK: standard postcode. Validated against serviceable_areas. | 'Please enter a valid ZIP/postal code' / 'Delivery not available to this address' |
               29. | country | Enum (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2) | Yes | Must be in supported countries list (US + EU/UK). | 'Please select a supported country' |
               30. | deliveryMethodId | UUID | Yes (step 2) | Must be a valid delivery method returned by calculate-totals API for this address. | 'Please select a delivery method' |
