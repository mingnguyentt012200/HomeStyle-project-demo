## M10 — Trade Portal Administration

### F-10.3 Account Manager Assignment & Workload Management

**Summary**

Trade Admins (and Super Admin) maintain the Account Manager (AM) roster, view per-AM workload, assign AMs to Verified Trade Accounts (initial assignment at approval time per F-10.1, plus reassignment as the book evolves), and rebalance books when AMs join, leave, or go on extended leave. The customer-facing surface (Verified Trade Account dashboard) displays the assigned AM's name, photo, and contact methods.

**Background / Context**

BRD §8.4 lists "assign account manager" as Phase-1 scope. Persona 2 (Interior Architect, EU) and Persona 3 (Corporate Buyer, US) are high-touch B2B segments — every Verified Trade Account is expected to have a named AM as a relationship single-point-of-contact. Without this admin surface, AM assignment is manual and reassignments break customer trust.

**User Story**

As a Trade Admin, I want to manage the AM roster and assign/reassign accounts in bulk so that every trade account has a named contact, no AM is overloaded, and book transitions during AM departures are smooth.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. Admin opens `/admin/trade/account-managers` (SCR-TR-05 / SCR-TA-07..09). Tabs: **Roster**, **Workload**, **Assignment Workbench**, **Audit**.
2. **Roster tab**: list of AMs with name, email, region, max-book-size, current-book-size, status (active / on-leave / departed), photo.
3. Admin **Add AM**: profile fields, region(s) covered, max-book-size, customer-facing email/phone (may differ from internal login), photo upload.
4. **Workload tab**: bar chart of book sizes vs max; flags AMs at >90% capacity; lists unassigned accounts (e.g. after AM departure).
5. **Assignment Workbench**: filter trade accounts by region / size / industry / current AM; bulk-assign or reassign with reason (e.g. AM departure, regional rebalance, customer escalation). Preview changes before commit.
6. On commit: customer-facing AM contact updated; optional notification email to affected customers (configurable per assignment).
7. AM lifecycle: **Set On-Leave** (book frozen, fallback AM optionally specified), **Mark Departed** (book becomes unassigned, surfaces on Workload tab and dashboard).
8. All write actions audited.

**Flow — Edge Cases**

| Edge Case | Expected System Behaviour |
|---|---|
| AM marked Departed with active book | Accounts moved to `unassigned`; alert on F-09.4 dashboard. Departed AM cannot be reassigned to. |
| Reassignment crosses regions | Allowed; warning shown if target AM's region does not include customer country. |
| Bulk reassignment exceeds target AM max-book | 422 with details; admin must split or raise cap. |
| Customer requests AM change | Recorded as reason `customer_request`; optional internal note linked to support ticket. |
| AM goes On-Leave with no fallback | Accounts remain assigned to the on-leave AM; UI surfaces all on-leave-without-fallback as a warning. |
| Concurrent assignment | Optimistic locking on each account row; per-row 409s reported in bulk result. |
| Customer-facing AM email contains an internal-only domain | Validation against allowed external domains list (Super Admin config). |

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | Every Verified Trade Account in `active` or `suspended` state must have an assigned AM, unless explicitly `unassigned` (post-departure). |
| BR-02 | Only Admin — Trade and Super Admin can create AMs, assign, or reassign. |
| BR-03 | Departed AMs are non-deletable; record retained for historical audit. |
| BR-04 | Reassignment requires a reason from controlled vocabulary (`am_departure`, `regional_rebalance`, `workload_balance`, `customer_request`, `escalation`, `other`). |
| BR-05 | Customer-facing AM email is distinct from internal login email and must pass external-domain validation. |
| BR-06 | AM `max_book_size` is advisory — bulk-assign hard-blocks at cap (BR-07), but individual escalation override available with Super Admin approval. |
| BR-07 | Bulk operations are transactional per-row, not all-or-nothing. Detailed result shows committed and rejected rows. |
| BR-08 | All write actions audited; bulk operations recorded with batch ID. |

**Input Validation Rules**

| Field | Type | Required | Validation Rules | Error Message Shown |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| name | String | Yes | 2–80 chars. | 'Name 2–80 chars.' |
| internal_email | Email | Yes | Valid; unique. | 'Email in use.' |
| customer_facing_email | Email | Yes | Domain in allowed list. | 'Domain not allowed for customer-facing email.' |
| customer_facing_phone | E.164 | No | E.164 format if set. | 'Phone must be E.164 format.' |
| regions | Array<ISO> | Yes | At least 1 country code. | 'Select at least one region.' |
| max_book_size | Integer | Yes | 1–500. | 'Max book size 1–500.' |
| status | Enum | Yes | `active`, `on_leave`, `departed` | 'Invalid status.' |
| reassignment_reason | Enum | Yes (reassign) | Vocabulary above | 'Select a reason.' |

**Open Questions**

- OQ-01: Should `notifyCustomer: true` always send email, or be Trade-Admin-default-on with override per assignment? Recommend default-on.
- OQ-02: Round-robin auto-assignment at approval time — Phase 1 manual only, or include simple round-robin within region? Recommend manual for Phase 1.
